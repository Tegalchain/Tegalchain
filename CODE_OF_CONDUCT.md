# Code of Conduct

Tegalchain is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community. By participating you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Provide helpful feedback and collaborate in good faith

Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior
- Personal attacks or harassment

Enforcement
- Reports should be sent to the project maintainers. Violations may result in warnings, removal from project channels, or banning from contribution.

For full details, contact the maintainers listed in GOVERNANCE.md.
