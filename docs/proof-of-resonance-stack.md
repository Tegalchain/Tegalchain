# Proof of Resonance (PoR) Stack

Tegalchain uses the Proof of Resonance (PoR) Stack as its consensus and impact verification architecture. PoR has three layers:

- PoRE — Proof of Regenerative Evidence: real-time telemetry and multi-source evidence collection, EQS and RIV scoring.
- PoREF — Proof of Resonance Foundation: validator network (Energy / Environmental / Community validators) and multi-signature consensus.
- PoREX — Proof of Resonance Exchange: impact tokenization, AMM/orderbook markets, retirement and accounting.

Green energy remains a mandatory requirement for block creation; PoR extends verification to all environmental attributes and ensures evidence quality and consensus confidence before minting impact tokens (tX*).
