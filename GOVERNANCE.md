# GOVERNANCE

Tegalchain operates as a DAO with role-based responsibilities and a merit-weighted voting system.

Roles
- Contributor: submits work and evidence
- Reviewer: reviews PRs and evidence
- Maintainer: merges changes and manages releases
- Council: elected body for high-level decisions and treasury governance

Voting & treasury
- TEGAL token governs protocol-level decisions; REGEN (utility) and tX* tokens represent impact/value.
- Treasury allocations are proposed and voted monthly.

Dispute resolution
- Community mediation and a Council appeal process. See CONTRIBUTING.md for escalation.
