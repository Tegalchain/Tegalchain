# Regenerative Finance Overview

Tegalchain measures regenerative impact with RFM (Regenerative Finance Metrics): EQS (Evidence Quality Score), RIV (Regenerative Impact Value), and Consensus Confidence. These metrics feed PoRE/PoREF decisions and PoREX market valuations.

tX* tokens represent synthetic, verifiable units of impact (see PoREX docs). The platform incentivizes high-quality evidence and long-term regenerative outcomes.
